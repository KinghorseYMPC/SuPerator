# Literature Taxonomy

This taxonomy supports literature metadata, literature cards, and concept
links. Tags are for general academic classification only and must not become
competition action guidance.

## 按研究对象分类

- `pde_theory`
- `operator_learning`
- `neural_operator`
- `physics_informed_ml`
- `scientific_machine_learning`
- `ai4s_agent`
- `benchmark_dataset`
- `numerical_methods`

- `hyperbolic_pde`
- `conservation_law`
- `reaction_diffusion`
- `fluid_dynamics`
- `wave_equation`
- `navier_stokes`
- `burgers_equation_general`

## 禁止使用的比赛攻略式标签

section or in compliance-audit examples:

- `task2_best_strategy`
- `score_boost`
- `leaderboard_strategy`
- `training_recipe_for_competition`
- `tuning_priority`
